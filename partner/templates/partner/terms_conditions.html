  • Student registration completed
  • Registration amount received by the Company
  • Student officially enrolled in the course

Payment Release Cycle

  • Commission payments are processed once every month.
  • Payment will be released on the 10th of every month.
  • Calculation period: 1st to last day of previous month.

Example:

  • Admissions between 1st January – 31st January
  • Commission released on 10th February

Payment Conditions

Commission will be paid only if:

  • Registration fee is successfully received in the company account.
  • No refund request is raised within the refund policy period.

If a student cancels or requests refund before batch start date, commission on that registration will NOT be payable.

In case of partial payment by the student, commission will be calculated proportionally as per received amount (as per company rules).

Mode of Payment

  • Bank Transfer (NEFT / IMPS / RTGS)
  • UPI (if applicable)

Partner must provide correct bank details in writing. Incorrect details may cause delay in payouts.

Dispute & Adjustment Policy

  • Any commission-related dispute must be raised within 7 days of payment release.
  • Company reserves the right to adjust excess or incorrect payments in the next payout cycle.

Partner Eligibility & Profile Policy

  • Partner must complete profile details before generating leads.
  • Bank details must be verified before commission payout.
  • Bank details can be updated only after proper approval process.

Bank Details Update Policy

For security reasons, bank details cannot be edited directly at any time.

To change bank information, partner must submit a valid reason (minimum 100 characters).

  • Request will be reviewed by admin team.
  • After approval, bank section will be unlocked.
  • Process may take up to 15 working days.

Providing false or misleading information may result in delay, rejection, or temporary suspension of payouts.

Policy Modification

The Company reserves the right to modify commission structure or payment cycle with prior written notice to partners.
